Comfortable Rooms at Sunday Hotel Wismar

Sunday Hotel Wismar offers 177 modern air-conditioned guest rooms, all equipped with ergonomic orthopedic mattresses, satellite TV, telephone, free Wi-Fi and a private bathroom with bathtub, WC, magnifying mirror and hairdryer. Barrier-free rooms are available on request.

Vacation with Your Pet

Sunday Hotel Wismar is a pet-friendly hotel. Your four-legged companion is welcome to join you on your Baltic Sea getaway.

Please inform us at the time of booking if you plan to bring a pet and let us know the size of your animal. An additional fee may apply per pet per night. Pets are not permitted inside the dining and wellness facilities.
