Wellness

Wissemare Wellness Area

After a day of exploring the coast or the charming streets of Wismar, there is no better place to unwind than the Wissemare wellness area at Sunday Hotel Wismar. Our leisure centre is open year-round and offers a wide range of facilities for guests of all ages.

Wellness Facilities

Relax & Recharge


Indoor Pool

Dive into relaxation in our spacious indoor pool. Whether you want a few refreshing lengths or simply want to float and unwind, our pool is the perfect escape regardless of the weather outside.

Sauna Area

Our sauna area includes a Finnish sauna, a log cabin sauna and a steam bath. Warm up, sweat it out and let the stresses of daily life melt away. Cozy bathrobe rental is available.

Jacuzzis & Rooftop Terrace

Treat yourself to a soak in one of our jacuzzis, available for an additional fee, or head up to the rooftop terrace for fresh air and views over the Mecklenburg landscape.

Fitness

Stay active during your stay in our well-equipped fitness studio. Keep up your workout routine and return home feeling refreshed and energized.

Good to Know

Your Wellness Escape

The Wissemare wellness area is open year-round and welcomes guests of all ages for indoor swimming, sauna relaxation, steam bathing, jacuzzi moments and fitness.

Please check current jacuzzi availability, bathrobe rental and any additional fees directly at reception.
