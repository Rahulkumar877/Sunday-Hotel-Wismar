Your Gateway to Wismar and the Baltic Coast

The Sunday Hotel Wismar is set in the picturesque coastal and lake landscape of Mecklenburg, just minutes from the gates of the historic Hanseatic city of Wismar. Surrounded by greenery and close to the Baltic Sea coast, it is ideal for relaxation, exploration and business events.

Accommodation

Comfortable Rooms with a Maritime Atmosphere

Choose from 177 modern air-conditioned guest rooms equipped with orthopedic mattresses, satellite TV, telephone, free Wi-Fi and private bathrooms with bathtub, WC, magnifying mirror and hairdryer. Barrier-free rooms are available on request.
