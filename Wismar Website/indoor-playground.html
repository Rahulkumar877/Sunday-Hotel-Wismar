Family Fun

Vacations for Young and Old

Sunday Hotel Wismar is a family-friendly hotel where both young guests and their parents can feel completely at home. From the Wissemare pool area to the outdoor children’s playground and indoor play corner, there is plenty to keep children entertained throughout the day.

Play Areas

Discover Family-Friendly Facilities


Wissemare Pool & Water Fun

Children will love the indoor pool at Sunday Hotel Wismar. Splash around, play and let the kids burn off some energy whatever the weather.

Children’s Playground

Our outdoor children’s playground provides a safe and fun space for younger guests while parents relax nearby.

Indoor Play Corner

The indoor play corner offers younger guests a welcoming space for games, creativity and relaxed family moments.

Family-Friendly Dining

Our restaurant offers children’s menus and high-chair service, with breakfast and dinner buffets that include plenty of options young guests will enjoy.

Families

A Relaxed Stay for the Whole Family

Sunday Hotel Wismar warmly welcomes guests of all ages. Families can enjoy spacious family rooms, children’s cots on request, the Wissemare pool area, outdoor play opportunities and family-friendly dining throughout the stay.

Dietary requirements can be accommodated on request.

Family Holidays in Wismar

Enjoy a family-friendly stay near the Baltic Sea coast with rooms, wellness, play areas and dining for all ages.
