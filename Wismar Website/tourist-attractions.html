Discover the Mecklenburg Baltic Coast

Sunday Hotel Wismar is perfectly positioned to explore the highlights of the Mecklenburg Baltic Coast region. From UNESCO-listed old towns and sandy beaches to cycling trails and charming lake landscapes, the area has something for every visitor.

Top Attractions

Discover the Region

Wismar – UNESCO World Heritage City

The historic Hanseatic city of Wismar is just 6 km from the hotel and is known for its medieval architecture, market square and lively harbor.

Baltic Sea Beaches

The nearest Baltic Sea beach is just 4 km from the hotel, while Boltenhagen seaside resort is approximately 19 km away.

Schwerin – City of Palaces

Visit Schwerin with its spectacular fairytale castle, charming old town, museums and renowned state theater.

phanTechnikum Wismar

An interactive science and technology museum with fascinating hands-on exhibitions for families and curious minds.

Cycling & Hiking

Well-marked trails lead through forests, along the coast and past picturesque villages around Wismar and Mecklenburg.
