Meetings, Conferences, Weddings & Celebrations

Sunday Hotel Wismar is the largest and most versatile conference hotel in the greater Wismar area. With nine flexible conference rooms accommodating up to 350 participants, the hotel is ideal for business meetings, seminars, training sessions, conferences, weddings and private celebrations.

All conference rooms benefit from natural daylight, modern AV equipment and dedicated support from our experienced events team.

Event Spaces

Flexible Conference Rooms

Choose from nine flexible conference rooms ranging from 42 m² to 343 m², suitable for meetings, seminars, training sessions and conferences.

Weddings & Celebrations

Celebrate your special day near the UNESCO World Heritage city of Wismar. Our events team supports setup, catering, floral decorations and accommodation for your guests.

Conferences & Business Events

From intimate team meetings to large-scale conferences, Sunday Hotel Wismar offers state-of-the-art facilities and professional support for smooth event delivery.
