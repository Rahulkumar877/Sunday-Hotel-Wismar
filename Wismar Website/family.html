Families

Vacations for Young and Old

Sunday Hotel Wismar is a family-friendly hotel. We want both our youngest guests and their parents to feel completely at home. From the Wissemare pool area to the outdoor children’s playground and a dedicated indoor play corner, there is plenty to keep young visitors entertained throughout the day.

Activities

Fun for the Whole Family


Wissemare Pool & Water Fun

Children will love the indoor pool at Sunday Hotel Wismar. Splash around, play and let the kids burn off some energy whatever the weather.

Children’s Playground

Our outdoor children’s playground provides a safe and fun space for younger guests while parents relax nearby.

Family Rooms

Spacious family rooms offer plenty of space for parents and children, with children’s cots available on request.

Family Comfort

Family-Friendly Dining

Our restaurant offers children’s menus and a high-chair service. The breakfast and dinner buffets include plenty of options that young guests will enjoy.

Dietary Requirements

We are happy to accommodate dietary requirements on request, so the whole family can enjoy a relaxed dining experience.

Family Holidays in Wismar

Plan your family stay near the Baltic Sea coast with rooms, wellness, play areas and dining for all ages.
