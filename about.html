Our Story

Sunday Hotel Wismar

A Hotel Surrounded by Mecklenburg Nature

Sunday Hotel Wismar is set in the picturesque coastal and lake landscape of Mecklenburg, just minutes from the gates of the historic Hanseatic city of Wismar. Surrounded by lush greenery and with quick access to the Baltic Sea coast, the hotel is an ideal starting point for discovering the beautiful north of Germany.

A Place for Relaxation, Families and Events

Whether you are seeking a peaceful wellness retreat, a family holiday by the Baltic Sea, a romantic break in one of Germany’s most charming UNESCO World Heritage cities, or a professional conference setting, Sunday Hotel Wismar offers everything you need under one roof.

Location

Wismar & the Mecklenburg Baltic Coast

Sunday Hotel Wismar is perfectly positioned for exploring the Mecklenburg Baltic Coast, from UNESCO-listed old towns and sandy beaches to cycling trails, lake landscapes and charming coastal destinations.

Nearby Attractions:

  • Wismar UNESCO World Heritage City – approx. 6 km
  • Nearest Baltic Sea beach – approx. 4 km
  • Boltenhagen seaside resort – approx. 19 km
  • Schwerin – City of Palaces
  • phanTechnikum Wismar

Why Guests Love Our Location

  • Close to the Baltic Sea coast and sandy beaches
  • Minutes from historic Wismar old town
  • Ideal for cycling, hiking and outdoor discovery
  • A peaceful base for families, couples and business travelers
What We Offer

Experience the Best of Sunday Hotel Wismar

Comfortable Accommodation

177 modern air-conditioned guest rooms with orthopedic mattresses, satellite TV, free Wi-Fi and private bathrooms.

Meetings & Events
