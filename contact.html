Getting Here

Travel to Sunday Hotel Wismar

Regular bus connections from the hotel reach Wismar city center in just a few minutes.

Sunday Hotel Wismar is located just minutes from the gates of historic Wismar, with quick access to the Baltic Sea coast and 160 parking spaces on-site, including 3 EV charging stations.

Wismar old town is approximately 6 km away, the nearest Baltic Sea beach is around 4 km away, and Boltenhagen seaside resort is approximately 19 km from the hotel.
